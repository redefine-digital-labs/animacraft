# Astral Courier prompt set

## Master

Create one polished original Japanese cel-shaded anime bust for a layered OC Maker: a gender-neutral celestial courier with violet eyes, midnight layered hair with cyan underside, an ivory-and-navy techwear jacket, and a small crescent clip. Use clean line art, two-step cel shadows, selective glossy highlights, a centered front-facing modular pose, full hair and shoulders, and a flat `#00ff00` chroma background. Avoid named artists, existing characters, logos, text, painterly rendering, photorealism, and 3D.

## Foundation atlas

Using the approved master as alignment reference, create a 2x2 atlas of blank bald head/neck/shoulder foundations in porcelain, warm beige, golden tan, and deep brown. Keep identical anatomy, no facial features, no hair, no clothing detail, and flat `#00ff00` outside the subject.

## Hair atlas

## Expression atlas

## Outfit atlas

## Accessory atlas
